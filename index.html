Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Vistara AI.

How does AI image generation work?
Our AI uses advanced machine learning models to transform text descriptions into high-quality images. Simply describe what you want to see, and our AI will create it for you.
What image formats are supported?
We support PNG and JPG formats. All images are generated in high resolution suitable for both web and print use.
Can I use the generated images commercially?
Yes! All images generated with Vistara AI can be used for both personal and commercial projects without restrictions.
How long does it take to generate an image?
Most images are generated in just a few seconds. Complex prompts may take slightly longer, but you'll have your image ready in under a minute.
